This past summer I spent working as a Google Engineering Practicum Intern in Cambridge, MA. I worked with an Infoextraction subteam of Knowledge. My project was geared to making our tools easier to use and set up. This project involved two major parts. The first consisted of writing a simplified UI for making new data templates, Schema, to be used for my team's internal tool. The second was laying the foundation for upgrades of Schema versions so that the data produced using the old data is not completely lost without the intervention of one of our team members.
